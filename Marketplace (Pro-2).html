Online Marketplaces


Online marketplaces are platforms where buyers and sellers come together to trade goods or services. Popular examples include:

  1. Global Platforms
    • Amazon: A vast range of products with global shipping.
    • eBay: Auction and fixed-price sales.
    • AliExpress:Affordable items, mostly from China.

  2. Regional Platforms
    • Flipkart:Popular in India.
    • Mercado Libre: Latin American countries.
    • Etsy: Handmade or vintage items.

  3. Niche Marketplaces
    • Fiverr: Freelance services.
    • Airbnb: Property rentals.
    • StockX: Sneakers and streetwear.
